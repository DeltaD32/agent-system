3D Office — Which Pixel Style?

All three are fully rotatable with orbit camera. Pick the aesthetic that feels right.

PAPER MARIO / OCTOPATH TRAVELER

Billboard Sprites in 3D

3D room, furniture, and floor — but agents are flat pixel sprites that always face the camera. Iconic "2.5D" look. Octopath Traveler / early Paper Mario style. Sprites stay crisp at any zoom.

✦ Best pixel art fidelity · Easiest to animate · Classic hybrid look

MINECRAFT / VOXEL STYLE

Voxel / Blocky 3D

Everything is cubes — room, desks, chairs, and agents. Minecraft/Crossy Road aesthetic. Chunky, bold, unmistakably pixel. Agents are little block figures with colored heads.

✦ Most playful · Very distinct · Easy to generate procedurally

LOW-POLY + PIXEL TEXTURES

Low-Poly + Pixel Textures

Smooth 3D geometry (room, desks, chairs) with pixel art textures baked on. Agents are simple 3D humanoid models with pixel art skins. PS1/N64 era aesthetic — modern indie game feel.

✦ Most cinematic · Full 3D agents · Closest to modern indie games

What all three share

✓ Three.js / Threlte renderer (Svelte)
✓ Orbit camera — drag to rotate, scroll to zoom
✓ A* pathfinding on 2D floor grid → 3D movement
✓ Click agent to open card / chat
✓ Real-time animation from WebSocket events
✓ Dynamic desk spawn/despawn on hire/dismiss

Which 3D style speaks to you? Can mix elements too (e.g. voxel agents in a low-poly room).
